First Word

No.01, 15 july '21

The first word. What was the first word ever said by a human being on earth? What was your children’s first word? Was it something to ‘summon’ you? Why was that? Was it because they needed you? How many times did they say that first word? When they were hungry? When did they fall after their first steps? When they hurt their knee after they fell from their bike the first time? Or when they were packing to leave you for the first time? What was your first word? Are you still saying it? When do you say it? Do you still need it? Will your children say it even after you die?
